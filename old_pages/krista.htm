Krista Hansen

Recruiter / Financial Aid Officer

Contact Krista at hansenk8@ferris.edu

Hello, my name is Krista Hansen originally from Battle Creek, I am now happy to live in St. Joseph near Lake Michigan's beautiful beaches. I earned my Bachelor of Music Education degree in 2003 and spent four years teaching instrumental music in the public schools.

Please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions regarding the admission or financial aid processes. If you'd like to set up a meeting with me, my office is conveniently located at our branch campus in Dowagiac on the campus of Southwestern Michigan College.

What is the one item you would encourage students to check out at Ferris before graduating?
Take a serious look at our study away programs. You have a chance to spend a semester or a year in another country while earning credits toward your degree. It is surprisingly affordable and a life changing experience!

When you are not recruiting students what do you do in your past time?
I enjoy music, writing, reading, and spending time with my friends and family.

What advice would you give to an incoming Freshman/Transfer Student?
Get involved with activities on campus. Whether you're into sports or music, politics or Greek life, there really is something for everyone here. You will meet people and enjoy your college experience so much more if you get involved in activities that interest you.

Favorite thing to do in Big Rapids?
Canoeing or floating in a tube down the Muskegon River with a group of friends.

Favorite Spot on Campus?
My favorite spot on campus is the Rock Dining Center. I have eaten at many different campus dining facilities and I will tell you that the food at Ferris is truly exceptional. You won't go hungry!


Territory:
Allegan, St. Joseph, Kalamazoo, Berrien, Cass, Van Buren & the state of Illinois
