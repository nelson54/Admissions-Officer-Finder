Leah Goode

Recruiting Officer

Contact Leah at goodel@ferris.edu

What makes Ferris standout over other schools?
Ferris stands out over other schools because of its focus on the students and the way the University prepares its students for careers, not just jobs.

What is the one item you would encourage students to check out at Ferris before graduating?
Students should check out all of the student organizations on campus-find something that interests you and get involved!

When you are not recruiting students what do you do in your past time?
My hobbies include reading, baking, cooking, and hiking. I also like to help out the FSU athletics department during volleyball and hockey games.

What advice you would give to an incoming Freshman/Transfer Student?
New FSU students need to attend all the orientation activities so they know about the great services and entertainment Ferris offers. Also, try and meet as many people as possible, and get out and try new things! College isn't just about learning in the classroom, a lot of learning takes place out of the classroom, too.

What is your favorite thing to do in Big Rapids?
Walking my dog at the Riverwalk.

What is your favorite spot on campus?
The Rock

Is there anything else we should know about you?
I've worked at a college or university for the last seven years, so I know that choosing the right college and major is very important. Working in higher education is very rewarding and it's great to see how a college education can open doors for graduates.


Territory:
Genesee, Lapeer, St. Clair, Oakland, Livingston & the state of Florida
