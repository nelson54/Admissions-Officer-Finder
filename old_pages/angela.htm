Angela Garrey

Recruiter / Financial Aid Officer

Contact Angela at garreya@ferris.edu

Why Did You Pick Ferris?
I picked Ferris for a numerous reasons, but the number one reason it is fit me! The mid size campus, the faculty and the community of Ferris made me feel at like I was coming home.

What is your favorite memory of Ferris?
My favorite memory of Ferris would probably be when I met my now husband (we have the same birthday).

What is your favorite tradition of Ferris?
My favorite tradition at Ferris would have to be all sorority rush. Watching young ladies open their bid cards is a tradition like no other it gives you a sense of pride of being in an organization with so many great young women.

What makes Ferris standout over other schools?
Ferris stands out compared to other schools with its peeps. I don't mean the candy, I mean all the amazing people who create the university. The students, the faculty, and the staff everyone you interact with on a day to day basis make you a better person and a better student.

The one item you encourage student to check out at Ferris before graduating?
The third floor of the Timme Center.

Advice you would give to an incoming Freshman/Transfer Student?
My advice to students coming in as a freshman or a transfer student is to use all the resources on campus available to you. Getting involved with the RSO's, going to sporting events, enjoying the restaurants of Big Rapids, knowing your faculty, taking a class SLA, find out more about the history of the institution, all of which are accessible to students.

What is your favorite thing to do in Big Rapids?
Going out to SCHUBERG'S with good friends, I have my own booth.

What is your Favorite Spot on Campus?
My favorite spot on campus would be the alumni building. It is where it all began and looking at the tile mosaic of the institutions true foundation is a great way to take pride in being a Ferris State Bulldog.
