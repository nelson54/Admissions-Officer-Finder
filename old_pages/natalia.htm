Natalia Carvalho-Pinto

Recruiting Officer

Contact Natalia at carv9@ferris.edu

Why did you pick Ferris?
Originally from Sao-Paulo, Brazil, I first came to Ferris as an International Student to study English for one year, but I quickly realized that Ferris was a great fit for me. I loved campus life and campus events, found my fit and made friends through the Student Organizations and Athletic Events, and learned that I could succeed academically here through all the resources available to students, such as our SLA program, tutoring center and career counseling. I am happy to say now that Ferris has been my home for the last 8 years. I love working with students and their families, and helping them take their first steps towards a college degree and their career goals.

What is your favorite memory at Ferris?
The Hockey games are great!

What is the one item you would encourage students to check out at Ferris before graduating?
A hockey game. The Rock dining facility. Homecoming week activities. The International Festival of Cultures.

What advice would you give to an incoming Freshman/Transfer Student?
Get Involved. Participate in as many student organizations and campus events as you are able to... There are great things to do at Ferris. Getting involved will make your college experience a lot more complete, more educational, fuller, and a lot more exciting!

What is your favorite thing to do in Big Rapids?
Eating at Buffalo Wild Wings, tubing, and taking my daughter to the local parks.

What is your favorite spot on campus?
The Ferris Library for Information Technology and Education (FLITE). It is one of the biggest, most complete and most beautiful libraries you will encounter in a higher education setting.
