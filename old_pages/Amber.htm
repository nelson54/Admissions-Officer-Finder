Amber Balmer

Recruiting Officer

Contact Amber at balmera@ferris.edu

Why Did You Pick Ferris? I really didn't pick Ferris it picked me! I moved here for a boy he wasn't the one but Ferris was...I was in love with FSU! I stayed for the PR program.

What is your favorite tradition at Ferris?
My favorite tradition at Ferris is Homecoming. It's the best time of year and I get to see all my long lost friends.

What makes Ferris standout over other schools?
Ferris is a fun loving community that works together. This school provides unique opportunities to all students and promotes individual growth. Ferris stands out above the rest because the amazing student body, faculty, and administration.

What is the one item you would encourage students to check out at Ferris before graduating?
The one thing a student must check out before they graduate from Ferris State is a Student Government Association meeting! Student Government is dear to my heart and provided me an "out of class room" education.

When you are not recruiting students what do you do in your past time?
In my free time I like to brush up on new trends in social media, read read read, cook, garden, and try new things like yoga!

What advice you would give to an incoming Freshman/Transfer Student?
My advice to an incoming student is take advantage of all the on campus activities and organizations. There is so much to do on campus... see the comedians, watch a movie in the quad, join a professional organization, be a student ambassador, eat pancakes until you fall over at Pancakes with the president, and have fun!!

Favorite thing to do in Big Rapids?
My favorite thing to do in Big Rapids...that's a tie between eating lemon sorbet in a waffle cone at the Old Pioneer Store and Tubing down the river in the middle of July! (I just had a thought I should try both at the same time)

Favorite Spot on Campus?
My favorite spot on campus is the clock tower at noon! It makes me proud to be a bulldog!
